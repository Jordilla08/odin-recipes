Pot Roast

Description

This is the most delicious Pot Roast you will EVER enjoy. Made with just a few simple ingredients and slow cooked in a crock pot, you are sure to fall in love!

Ingredients

  • Chuck Roast
  • Au Jus Gravy Mix
  • Ranch Gravy Mix
  • Jarred Pepperoncini Peppers
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Place the chuck roast in the bottom of your crock pot.
  2. Sprinkle the ranch and au jus mixes over the top of the roast.
  3. Add the butter and pepperoncini peppers.
  4. Place the lid on top and cook over low heat for 8 hours or more.
