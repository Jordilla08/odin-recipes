Pan seared Steak

Description

As everyone is staying home, working from home and homeschooling, people are cooking way more often and looking to improve their cooking skills. You all have been asking for more simple and delicious recipes that come together fast and have minimal ingredients. This steak recipe is so satisfying and will impress your entire family.

Ingredients

  • 2 New York Strip steaks, or 2 Top Sirloin steaks
  • 1/2 tbsp vegetable oil, or any high heat cooking oil
  • 1 1/2 tsp of sea salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp of unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves of garlic, peeled and cut into quarters
  • 1 sprig of fresh rosemary

Steps

  1. Thoroughly pat steak dry with a paper towel. Just before cooking, generously season with 1 1/2 tsp salt and 1 tsp black pepper
  2. Heat a cast iron pan and add 1/2 tbsp oil over medium-high heat. Once heated add steak to the skillet. Sear for about 4 minutes until a brown crust has formed the flip and repeat. Using tongs, turn the streak on its sides to render the white fat and sear the edges (1 minute per edge)
  3. Reduce heat to medium and immediately add 2 tbsp of butter, quartered garlic cloves and rosemary. Baste the sauce over the steak and continue for 1 minute.
